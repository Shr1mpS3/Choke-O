Choke-O

The Choke-O Company was founded by Mr. Mhari Gregorio. In our unique and delicious chocolates, he is passionate about capturing the flavors of the Philippines. In addition to satisfying his own taste buds, he wanted to produce something that would provide his consumers a special and memorable experience. Regardless of their age, background, or dietary preferences, he wanted to make a product that anybody could enjoy.


Our CEO, Mr. Nathan Santiago, shares the same passion for developing premium goods that accurately capture the rich and complex flavors of the Philippines. He thinks that chocolate is an artistic medium that can be used to convey a story, express a culture, and make an unforgettable experience in addition to being a tasty treat. He is dedicated to creating Choke-O as a chocolate brand that is not only delectable but also a tribute to the Filipino culture and flavor, recreated in a way that will thrill everyone who tastes it. Choke-O is such a high-end chocolate business because of its CEO's dedication to perfection and quality in all he does.


Along with our CEO, we also have a group of talented, committed, and expert chocolate producers. Being led by our chief supervisor, Mr. Zhandy Dacula, a skilled chocolatier with more than 10 years of expertise in the chocolate manufacturing sector. He is an expert at producing premium chocolates that not only taste fantastic but also stand out from the competition because of their unique flavor profiles. Together, we aspire to build a chocolate company that delivers a wide range of goods as well as an experience that is unlike anything our customers have ever had.
