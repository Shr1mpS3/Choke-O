Vision

Our goal at Choke-O is to produce great chocolate products that not only satisfy Filipino consumers' cravings but also highlight the artistic prowess and craftsmanship of our community's craftspeople. In the future, we see Choke-O being a revered and reliable luxury chocolate brand that is connected to excellent chocolate and a superior taste.in the Philippines and in the world.


Mission

Our mission at Choke-O is to create mouth-watering chocolates using only the finest ingredients and techniques. From the cocoa beans to the final product, we take great care in ensuring that every step of the process is done with precision and care to create a taste that's truly extraordinary. We want to inspire chocolate lovers to savor the richness of each bite and treasure the memories of each consumption experience. We also aim to support and empower local artisan communities, ensuring that everyone involved in the chocolate-making process is treated with respect and dignity.
